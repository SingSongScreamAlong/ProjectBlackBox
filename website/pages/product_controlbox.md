## Capabilities (Feature Cards)

### Incident Detection
Automatic detection and categorization of on-track incidents as they happen.

### Fault Attribution
Multi-driver fault analysis with confidence scoring and evidence trails.

### Rulebook Engine
Upload your league rules — ControlBox auto-governs based on your specific regulations.

### Post-Race Reports
Comprehensive, printable reports with incident breakdowns and penalty recommendations.

### Analytics Suite
Heatmaps, timelines, and statistical analysis across races and seasons.

### Stewards Assistant
AI-powered recommendations to help human stewards make faster, fairer decisions.

---

## How ControlBox Works

### Step 1: Ingest
ControlBox ingests telemetry data and replay files from your races.

### Step 2: Classify
The rulebook AI analyzes incidents and classifies outcomes based on your league rules.

### Step 3: Report
Export detailed, shareable reports with visualizations and penalty recommendations.
