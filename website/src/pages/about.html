Our Mission

We build AI systems that make racing more human. Tools that improve clarity, reduce overwhelm, and help drivers and leagues focus on what matters.

Racing deserves more than silence and guesswork. Every driver deserves the support of a full team. Every league deserves fair, consistent race control. That's what we're building.

The Founder

One Lap at a Time

okboxbox was founded by Conrad, whose journey through PTSD led to an unexpected discovery: sim racing as a tool for focus, presence, and recovery. What started as personal therapy became a mission — Post Traumatic Racing.

"The 'one lap at a time' philosophy isn't just a tagline. It's a practice of staying present, making clear decisions under pressure, and finding flow in the chaos."

okboxbox exists to give every racer the tools to experience that clarity. Whether you're chasing your first win or managing a 200-driver league, you deserve the support to focus on what matters.

The Ecosystem

Everything in the okboxbox suite is designed to work together seamlessly. PitBox handles the driver experience. ControlBox handles the league experience. Future modules will extend into coaching, telemetry analysis, team management, and beyond.

One ecosystem. Many boxes. All racing.
